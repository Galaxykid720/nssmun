About NSSMUN

Nee Soon South Model United Nations (NSSMUN) is a prestigious MUN conference dedicated to fostering diplomacy, global awareness, and critical thinking among students. Held in the vibrant district of Nee Soon South, our conference brings together passionate young minds from around the world to engage in high-level discussions on global affairs. We provide a platform for delegates to develop their research, public speaking, and negotiation skills in a professional and inclusive environment.

Committees

United Nations Security Council (UNSC)

Deliberating on pressing global security threats, conflicts, and international peacekeeping efforts. Delegates will engage in crisis simulations and diplomatic negotiations.

World Health Organization (WHO)

Focusing on global health challenges such as pandemic preparedness, vaccine distribution, and healthcare access in developing nations.

United Nations Human Rights Council (UNHRC)

Discussing fundamental human rights issues, refugee crises, and policies to protect marginalized communities worldwide.
